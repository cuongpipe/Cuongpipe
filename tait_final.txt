Topic 7: Current Technology Trends

Artificial Intelligence (AI)
Description:
Artificial Intelligence is a technology that helps machines process data, learn and make decisions like humans.
Current examples:
Currently, AI is being used in ChatGPT, Google Translate, facial recognition, self-driving cars, data analysis.
Future predictions:
In the next 5–10 years, AI will help programmers write and test code. In the next 20 years, AI can automatically create complete software.
Impact:
AI will change the way I work, helping to increase productivity but also requiring new skills.
How to adapt:
I will learn Python, learn AI libraries like TensorFlow, and do small projects related to AI to understand how it works.

Cloud Computing
Description:
A technology that allows data to be stored and processed over the Internet, instead of using a local computer.
Current example:
Currently, services such as Google Drive, iCloud, GitHub, and platforms such as AWS, Azure are very popular.
Future prediction:
In the next 5–10 years, most software will run on the cloud. Deployment and maintenance will be tied to the cloud.
Impact:
I need to know how to write cloud compatible software, understand security and deploy remote systems.
How to adapt:
You will learn to use AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes, and practice deploying small personal applications to the cloud.

Criteria for choosing a computer for gaming:
The computer must have a powerful Core i7 CPU or higher, a discrete graphics card (GPU), 16GB of RAM or higher, and a screen with a high refresh rate of 120Hz or higher. If it is a laptop, battery life is not too important because it is often plugged in when playing games.
One has a 20 million VND budget for gaming. I recommended the MSI GF63, with Core i7, 16GB RAM, and GTX 1650. It runs games smoothly but has short battery life (3–5 hours).

Selection criteria for office work:
The computer only needs a core i3 or core i5 cpu. Only needs 8GB RAM or more. The screen also only needs 60hz. If it is a laptop, then, Good battery, light machine to easily carry to school or work remotely
The other has a 10 million VND budget for office work. I suggested the Acer Aspire 5, with Ryzen 5, 8GB RAM, and SSD. It runs office tasks well and has good battery life (6–8 hours).

Choosing the right laptop depends on your purpose. Gaming needs strong specs, while office tasks need stability and long battery life. Always consider your budget and usage carefully.
